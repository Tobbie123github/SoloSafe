How SoloSafe Works

Simple steps to keep you safe on every adventure

1. Create Your Trip

Enter your destination, travel dates, and set up your safety check-in schedule.

2. Set Check-ins

Choose your preferred check-in interval from 1 minute to 48 hours.

3. Add Emergency Contacts

Add trusted contacts who will be notified if you miss a check-in.

4. Stay Connected

Receive timely notifications to check in and confirm you're safe.

5. Emergency Support

Trigger SOS alerts instantly if you need help. Contacts receive your location.

6. Travel with Confidence

Enjoy your adventure knowing help is always just one tap away.
